About Us

What is Gotchu MRS ?

Gotchu MRS is a music recommender system developed for a final year project using Spotify's Web API. It aims to generate more accurate recommendations with different algorithms and machine learning.


What makes it different ?

There are a lot of similar applications today, but what makes Gotchu different is that other applications directly takes the recommendations from Spotify while we use algorithms to filter the results to make recommendations for you.

How to use?

Users can either choose to login with their Spotify account and navigate to the profile section where recommendations are generated based on your own music preferences, like favourite tracks and playlists. For those who chooses not to, you can enjoy the features in the home page where recommendations are made based on either the song, artist or genre you entered.
